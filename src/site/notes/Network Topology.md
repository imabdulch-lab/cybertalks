The physical or logical arrangement of nodes (devices like computers, servers, and routers) and connections (cables or wireless links) in a computer network.

There are 2 types of Network Topology.
1. [[Physical Topology\|Physical Topology]]
2. [[Logical Topology\|Logical Topology]]
